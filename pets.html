Pets!

  • Junior

    Junior was the first addition to our family in 2016. Though loveable, Junior is very territorial with strangers and may attack the feet. When it's time for breakfast or dinner, he opens the door to make sure his father can easily bring his meals to him.

  • Pigasus

    Pigasus is more shy than Junior and tends to hide away from visitors. He is more of a wild-child than his brother, often venturing outside to "root" for snacks.

  • Meow Meow

    Meow Meow is the most recent addition to the family, adopting us in 2021 just before the atmospheric river. Although exhibiting physical traits of a feline, Meow Meow is more like a dog, preferring to cuddle with her parents.
